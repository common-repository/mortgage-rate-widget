== Description ==

This plugin lets you embed a  [Mortgage Rates Widget](http://www.freerateupdate.com/mortgage-rate-widget "Mortgage Rates Widget") onto your WordPress weblog. Free mortgage rate widget includes 30 year fixed Conforming, FHA and Jumbo mortgage rates. It's updated daily by [FreeRateUpdate.com](http://www.freerateupdate.com/ "FreeRateUpdate.com") who researches wholesale lender's rate sheets. The color is customizable to match your blog. 



== Installation ==



1. Upload plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I customize widget's look and feel? =

Just add the widget to your sidebar, and you'll be able to customize it in the wigdet settings.
